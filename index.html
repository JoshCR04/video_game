History
Eldric, a young adventurer of unknown origin, harbors an ancient power in his blood of which he is barely aware. Since childhood, he has felt a strange attraction to a mythical sword that, according to ancient prophets, only he can wield. This weapon, lost in the legends of ancient times, is the key to liberating a kingdom that has fallen under the dark veil of evil and despair.

The kingdom of Astralis, once a land of prosperity and beauty, has been corrupted by dark magic that emanates from its depths. Nature has withered, the once fertile fields and forests now lie barren, and the creatures that were once allies of humans have turned into twisted beasts sowing chaos. The skies are perpetually darkened by magical storms, and the people live in constant fear and despair, trapped in a nightmare from which they cannot awaken.

The origin of all this evil seems to trace back to a legendary sorcerer, a wizard known as Zalrath, whose power has grown to such an extent that he controls every corner of the kingdom from his imposing castle. It is said that Zalrath, along with his most loyal guardians—a group of beings as ancient and dark as the very curse that afflicts Astralis— has spent centuries absorbing the life essence of every living creature to prolong his life and increase his power. With each passing day, more souls are consumed, and his control over reality grows stronger.

Guided by a prophecy that speaks of a warrior destined to wield the lost sword, Eldric embarks on a solitary mission. Armed only with his courage and the mysterious power flowing through his veins, he ventures into lands plagued by monsters and sinister enemies, determined to find the key before it's too late.
